GPE, short for Great Park Explorations, is a website based on creepy and unsettling encounters centered around Newcastle Great Park, in Gosforth.

The locations, images, information & stories are community-submitted and require approval from the founder. You can submit a story or location . Inputting fake or extremely disturbing stories or locations will result in an IP ban from submitting, which can be appealed .

All submissions are monitored and may be reported to authorities if they involve illegal activity, threats, or unsafe behavior. GPE promotes curiosity and exploration, but safety and legality come first.
